MARIKA NIKO

Marika Niko is a mover, organizer, and designer. She is from Japan, but has been a nomad for the longest years moving from Thailand to the U.K., Japan to the Netherlands, and the U.S. to the United Arab Emirates. There, she is currently pursuing her B.A. in Theater at New York University Abu Dhabi.
Marika has been trained in classical ballet. Most recently, she has shifted her focus to the philosophy and practice of Butoh.
