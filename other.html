Spectra
2019 Workshop for Script Development
Written and directed by Simon Wilkes. Conceived as a 'comicbook- play,' and developed through a series of rehearsal laboratories and writing workshops, Spectra seeks to adopt the visual form of comicbooks as a means storytelling to the form of theatre in order to explore script writing as a collaborative process and theatre as a form of writing research into a mode of playwrighting whereby text functions as an icon of language as well as image, as it does in the form of comicbooks..
