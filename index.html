MARIKA NIKO

Hi, I am Marika! I am a second year student in New York University Abu Dhabi, currently studying Theater. Performing, including both acting and dancing, has been my focus for the longest time. My recent research includes the practice and philosophy of Butoh.
At the same time, my recent work lies more on the design aspect of theater-making, primarily costume and scenic design.
I have expanded my skills over the past year, where I gained costume-making/sewing skills, as well as programming skills in HTML, CSS, Arduino, and Python as an attempt to produce different forms of theater.
Please check my work both on-stage and off-stage.
